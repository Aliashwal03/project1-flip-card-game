How To Play

1. Objective:
Find all the matching pairs of cards by flipping them two at a time.

2. How to Play:
- Click on a card to flip it.
- Then click on a second card to try to find its match.
- If both cards match, they will be highlited.
- If they don't match, they flip back.

3. Win Condition:
The game ends when all card pairs are matched correctly or the time is over.
